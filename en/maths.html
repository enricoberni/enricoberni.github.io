Mathematics

Here, you will find some of my mathematical work. I am mainly interested in geometry and topology, but I also like to meddle in functional analysis and mathematical logic.

Here are some of my main iterests:

  • Bounded cohomology;
  • \(\ell^1\)-homology and simplicial volume;
  • \(L^2\)-cohomology;
  • Hyperbolic geometry;
  • Negatively curved manifolds;
  • Anti-de Sitter geometry;
  • Geometric group theory;
  • Amenability of groups / Analytic group theory.
